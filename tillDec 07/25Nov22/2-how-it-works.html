Launch Architecture

Organized by Jobs-to-Be-Done

Launch Copilot structures launch work around the actual jobs pharmaceutical teams need to accomplish‚Äînot generic project management categories. This Jobs-to-Be-Done framework ensures teams have the right tools, templates, and guidance for each distinct type of launch work.

Two Categories of Launch Work: Functional workstreams create the deliverables and execute launch tactics (medical education, commercial campaigns, payer strategies). Oversight workstreams ensure cross-functional alignment, quality standards, and strategic coherence. Both are essential‚Äîand Launch Copilot supports each with purpose-built capabilities.
